### Description
Count with Kids transforms everyday math practice into a colorful adventure — with friendly dinosaurs, magical unicorns, and trophies to earn.

From counting on fingers to solving problems like a superhero, your child will build confidence with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division — all while having a blast.

KEY FEATURES

- Four operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division in any combination
- Adjustable difficulty: counting ranges from 10 to 1,000, scaling with your child's abilities
- Instant feedback: correct answers appear immediately so kids learn from mistakes in real time
- Clean Sheet celebrations: special reward animations for perfect scores
- Streak system: earn silver medals and gold cups for consecutive perfect rounds
- Progress dashboard: bar charts tracking errors, completion times, and daily streaks
- Five themes: Dinosaur, Unicorn, Penguin, plus unlockable Lion and custom Emoji themes
- Hidden mascot challenge: tap the mascot for a surprise head-to-head counting race!
- Customizable sessions: 1–10 problems per page with optional countdown timer
