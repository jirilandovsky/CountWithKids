Count with Kids makes math practice fun and engaging for children! With colorful themes, customizable difficulty, and a built-in performance dashboard, your child will love practicing addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

FEATURES:

- Four operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division
- Adjustable counting ranges: up to 10, 20, 100, or 1000
- Configurable number of problems per session (1-10)
- Optional countdown timer to add a fun challenge
- Instant feedback with correct answers shown for mistakes
- "Clean Sheet" celebrations for perfect scores

THREE PLAYFUL THEMES:
Choose from Dinosaur, Unicorn, or Penguin themes — each with unique colors and mascot to keep kids motivated.

PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD:
Track progress over time with visual bar charts showing average errors, average time, and clean sheet streaks. Filter by day, week, month, or year.

THREE LANGUAGES:
Full support for English, Czech, and Hebrew — including right-to-left layout for Hebrew.

PRIVACY FIRST:
No data collection, no ads, no internet connection required. All progress is stored locally on the device.

Designed for children ages 4 and up. Simple, distraction-free interface that lets kids focus on learning.
