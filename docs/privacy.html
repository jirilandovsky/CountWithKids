Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Count with Kids is a children's math practice app. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Count with Kids:

  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not access the camera, microphone, location, or contacts
  • Does not include any third-party SDKs or frameworks

Data Storage

All app data (settings and practice session history) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for children ages 4 and up. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide by simply not collecting any data.

Third-Party Services

Count with Kids does not integrate with any third-party services. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no external network requests.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Since we collect no data, changes would only reflect updates to this document's language.
