Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the theme?

Go to the Settings tab and tap on your preferred theme: Dinosaur, Unicorn, or Penguin.

How do I change the language?

Go to the Settings tab and select your preferred language: English, Czech, or Hebrew.

How do I adjust the difficulty?

In Settings, you can change:

  • Counting range (up to 10, 20, 100, or 1000)
  • Math operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division)
  • Number of problems per page (1-10)
  • Optional countdown timer

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the internet. If you delete the app, your practice history will be removed.

Is this app safe for children?

Yes. Count with Kids collects no data, has no ads, no in-app purchases, and requires no internet connection. It is designed for children ages 4 and up.
