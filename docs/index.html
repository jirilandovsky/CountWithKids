Print. Practice. Scan. Done.

Math practice doesn't have to be screen time.

With the unique Print & Scan feature, your child can work on printed worksheets with a pencil — just like at school. When they're done, simply scan the sheet with the app's camera. The answers are checked automatically, scores are tracked, and rewards are earned — all without your child needing to sit in front of a screen.

Print

Generate a worksheet and print it out.

Solve

Your child writes answers by hand — screen-free practice.

Scan

Point the camera at the completed sheet — results are recorded instantly.
